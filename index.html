Frequently Asked Questions

How do you make money?

We charge $2 for every gift we send out. We don't mark up gift prices and charge you exactly what it costs us to purchase, pack and ship.

Where can I find a list of gifts you can send out?

A general list is in the Gift Examples Section. Just ask us and we can provide a full list of possibile gifts.

Can you send gifts that aren't on your list?

If we can purchase it on the internet, we can likely buy, ship and track it for you. Call us.

How do I let you know which clients get which gifts?

You'll enter that info in your dashboard after signing up. You can choose which clients get specific gifts.

I'm worried you might take my client info and do nefarious things with it.

That's a fair thought. We take privacy seriously and don't use contact info for anything other than sending gifts. We don't sell or share the lists with anyone. We don't call or upsell people on the list. You're clients never know we exist. If there is a delivery problem, we'll contact you first.

How to report issue / feedback?

You can call us. We'd love to hear from you.
